Welcome to Joni's Blathers - a Pseudoblog. It's a blog in the sense that the texts here are informal like you'd find on a blog; it's a pseudo in the sense that this lacks the neat temporal organization blogs tend to have and it's too late to impose it on the texts now.

There're texts in English and in Finnish (suomi). Click on the checkboxes below to select what kind of stuff you'd like to see. If you see anything interesting, click on the link below the title and taglist and it'll open the text in a new window.
