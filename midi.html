My arrangement of the folk song "Konevitsan kirkonkellot". I think I heard it for the first time in elementary school, when some local folk band performed in in some assembly, with kantele, accordion and so on. Some remnants of the versions by Piirpauke and Sentenced have been assimilated into this one:

Konevitsan kirkonkellot

An alternative melody for the hymn 124 from the Finnish Evangelical Lutheran hymn book (https://virsikirja.fi/virsi-124-kay-koyhaan-sydameeni/). The start of the melody is a clear allusion to the melody in the hymn book, but it then diverges from that. For the second part the melody switches to the dominant key. It's really simple melody, since I was supposed to sing it myself, and my singing skills aren't that great. The last phrase of each verse is repeated twice. In this MIDI version there's just three verses:
